Wordpress to Hugo

Great text from Christopher Kirk-Nielsen about how to convert blog from Wordpress to Hugo. I recommend reading this to anyone who plans to make the ‘move’ :)

WordPress has a pretty robust architecture that can feel a bit too complex to run a simple blog. @ckirknielsen sheds light on how Hugo can help you create a blog that is simple and fast.
